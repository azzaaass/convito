Convito brings the flavors of Renaissance France and Italy to life, inspired by the legendary culinary masters Bartolomeo Scappi and Marie-Antoine Carême.

Convito is more than a meal – it's a journey through time where every dish tells a story, and every flavor recreates the artistry of centuries past. Experience the elegance of Renaissance dining reimagined for the modern palate.
